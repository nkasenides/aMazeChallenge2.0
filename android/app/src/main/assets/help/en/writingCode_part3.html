Pickable items

Some items will be created inside the maze randomly. Each of these items has specific effects (either good or bad) and are picked when your avatar walks over them.

These items can affect your health, points or speed inside the maze which make it crucial to know what each item is to either take or avoid it.

Fruits (Rewards)

  • Oranges (+50 health)
  • Apples (+30 health)
  • Grapes (+25 health)
  • Watermelons (+20 health)
  • Bananas (+15 health)
  • Strawberries (+10 health)
  • Peaches (+5 health)

Coins & Giftbox (Rewards)

  • 5pt Coin (+5 points)
  • 10pt Coin (+10 points)
  • 20pt Coin (+20 points)
  • Giftbox (+50 points)

Other rewards

  • Speedhack (Move twice at each turn for the next 5 turns)

Penalties

  • Trap (No moves for the next 5 turns)
  • Bomb (Causes player to lose health when it explodes)
